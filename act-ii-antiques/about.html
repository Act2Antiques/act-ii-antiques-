Our Story

Act II Antiques was founded on a simple belief: that beautiful objects deserve to be cherished across generations. Every piece in our collection has lived a life before finding its way to us, and we take pride in helping each item find its next chapter.

Our passion for antiques began decades ago, sparked by the discovery of a Victorian pocket watch at a local estate sale. That single find ignited a lifelong journey of seeking out remarkable pieces with stories to tell.

Today, we travel throughout the country visiting estate sales, auctions, and private collections to source only the finest antiques.
